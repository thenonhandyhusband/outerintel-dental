5. Your 90-Day Action Plan

Prioritized tasks based on competitive gap size and estimated effort to implement. Start with Quick Wins to build momentum.

Quick Wins Week 1–2  ·  Low effort, high signal
  • Set up a 24-hour Google review response policy — respond to all new reviews within 24 hours, both positive and negative
  • Add "gentle dentist" and "we explain everything" to your Google Business Profile description — use the exact patient language from 5-star reviews
  • Audit your appointment confirmation flow — add an estimated wait time note so patients arrive with calibrated expectations
Medium-Term Month 1–2  ·  Structured effort required
  • Launch a post-visit review request via SMS or email — target 2+ new reviews per week to begin closing the velocity gap with CrossTown Dental
  • Create a dedicated "Dental Implants" page on your website with procedure info, cost range, and FAQs — CrossTown is currently capturing this search traffic
  • Add same-day emergency appointment availability to your Google Business Profile and homepage — only 1 competitor currently claims this positioning
Strategic Month 2–3  ·  Investment with longer payoff
  • Test a teeth whitening promotion or bundle — Smile Studio ATX is winning this service segment in your market and accumulating reviews on it
  • Reach out to 2 primary care physicians in your area about referral partnerships — competitor reviews show a meaningful share of patients arrive via MD referral
  • Brief your front desk team on the #1 complaint pattern across all practices — unexplained wait times — and build a simple wait acknowledgment script
